Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2024 · Masa Enterprise · Namanve/Mukono, Uganda

🔒

Absolute Zero-Data Collection Guarantee

Masa Enterprise does not collect, store, process, sell, share, or monetise any personal data about visitors to this website. There are no analytics trackers, no advertising pixels, no cookies, no fingerprinting scripts, no session recording tools, and no third-party data brokers involved in operating this website.

This is not a policy written to satisfy a checkbox. It reflects a genuine design decision: this site is a static informational platform. We have no business model that depends on your data.

1. What We Do NOT Collect

  • IP addresses, device identifiers, or browser fingerprints
  • Cookies, local storage values, or tracking beacons
  • Behavioural data, click paths, scroll depth, or session recordings
  • Location data, time-on-page, or referral sources
  • Email addresses unless you explicitly send us an email
  • Any form of user telemetry or metadata profile

2. Contact Data You Voluntarily Provide

When you email us at masadavid010@gmail.com or message via WhatsApp (+256 764 411 376), you are initiating direct communication with Masanso David. That communication is governed by Google's and Meta's respective privacy policies for their platforms. Masa Enterprise uses this information solely to respond to your inquiry and scope your project. It is never sold, shared, or archived beyond active project delivery.

3. Third-Party Services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts (googleapis.com). Google may log the font request. Beyond this, no third-party scripts, SDKs, CDNs, or ad networks are loaded. There are no embedded social media widgets. The Google Maps links on our Contact page are standard outbound links — clicking them opens Google's own platform, governed by Google's privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

Zero data is retained by the website infrastructure itself. Project correspondence retained in email/WhatsApp threads is deleted upon project completion or at the client's request, whichever is sooner.

5. Your Rights

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete. If you have any concern, contact masadavid010@gmail.com directly and Masanso David will respond personally within 48 hours.

6. Policy Updates

This policy will be updated if our data practices ever change. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision. We will not retroactively change how previously collected data is handled — because there is none.
