Power Backup

Keep Your Network
Always On.

Mini DC UPS systems that keep routers, switches, and access points online during power outages. Specifically engineered for MikroTik, Ubiquiti, and TP-Link gear on Uganda's variable power grid.

Why DC UPS?

Built for Uganda's Power Reality

⚡

Zero Transfer Time

DC UPS systems switch to battery in under 5ms — routers never reboot, VPN tunnels stay up, active sessions are uninterrupted.

🔋

Router-Specific Voltage

Outputs matched to 12V or 24V DC devices — no power brick, no AC inversion losses, longer runtime per Ah of battery.

📏

Compact Form Factor

Mounts in-line with your existing power supply. No bulky UPS cabinet — fits in any rack or equipment closet cleanly.
