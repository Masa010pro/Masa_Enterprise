About Masa Enterprise

The Engineer
Behind the Platform

Masa Enterprise is the practice of Masanso David — a multi-discipline IT professional who designs, builds, and maintains production-grade systems across networking, software, and mobile engineering.

Founder Profile

Masanso David

Based in the Namanve/Mukono area of Uganda, Masanso David founded Masa Enterprise to provide enterprise-grade IT services to businesses and individuals that traditionally couldn't access them. He operates across four technical verticals simultaneously: network infrastructure, full-stack software, Android engineering, and tech education.

Every engagement is handled personally or as a team depending on the size of the project to ensure reliability, quality and timely delivery of any services requested.
